Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is "Ethical Hacking"?

Ethical Hacking (or Penetration Testing) is the authorized, simulated cyberattack on a system to identify its security vulnerabilities. Unlike malicious hacking, the goal is to find and fix weaknesses before a real attacker can exploit them.

My Facebook account was hacked. Can you get it back?

While no one can guarantee 100% recovery, I specialize in the official recovery processes. I assist by guiding you through the correct channels, documenting evidence, and helping you secure your account properly once recovered to prevent future incidents.

How long does a security audit take?

The timeframe depends on the scope of the project. A basic social media audit might take a day, while a full network penetration test for a small business could take several days to a week. I will provide a clear time estimate after our initial consultation.
